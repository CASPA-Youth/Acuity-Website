Canada's largest high school Hackathon

Join over 200 high school students in Waterloo to create unique tech projects as JAMHacks celebrates its 10th birthday! Get ready for a JAM-packed weekend of building, learning, and collaboration. Don't forget to bring your ideas and curiosity to build something celebration-worthy and compete for the top spot! Ready to RSVP for the party this June?

Bring Ideas To Life

Build an app, game, website, robot, or anything you can dream up! After 36 hours of building, brainstorming, and coding, you'll unwrap your projects to judges and compete for prizes!

Form Connections

Connect with high school students from all across Canada, meet industry experts, and learn from mentors in the tech community. At JAMHacks, hackers of all skill levels and experiences are welcome!

Create Lasting Memories!

Bring your party spirit and gear up for a weekend filled with fun activities, free meals, and one-of-a-kind swag! We've got a spread of activities planned for the entire weekend. Celebrate a decade of JAMHacks with us!

The University of Waterloo continues to support JAMHacks, building the next generation of innovators, entrepreneurs, and creators. Step into Canada's largest engineering school — a pipeline for engineering talent for the world's leading companies. Ranked among the top 50 engineering schools globally, the school's reputation for excellence is built on the foundation of co-op education and a bold history of innovation.

Frequently Asked Questions

A hackathon is where student innovators come together for a weekend of building, learning, and creating something entirely their own. When the countdown ends, you'll pitch your project and compete for exciting prizes. Along the way, you can level up your skills in hands-on workshops, connect with mentors, and enjoy fun mini-activities. It's your chance to explore new ideas, experiment with tech, and be part of a vibrant, collaborative community.

Yes, JAMHacks is completely free to attend! Thanks to our sponsors, meals, snacks, and SWAG will all be provided throughout the weekend. Just bring your creativity (and your appetite) and we'll take care of the rest.

Your project can be on anything you can dream up as long as it involves technology in some way. Whether it's something practical, or something more creative, this is your chance to build something you're excited about. The only limit is your imagination!

We'll keep the event organized on Discord, that's where all the big announcements, updates, and mentor connections will happen. The full schedule will be released closer to the event, and we'll share real-time highlights and reminders on Instagram. Stay tuned so you don't miss a thing!

Plus, hackathons need designers, presenters, organizers, and big-picture thinkers meaning that there's a place for everyone on a team.

We're inviting all high school students to join the JAMHacks 10 celebrations! If you're outside that group, you can still join the celebration as a mentor or judge and help guide the next generation of innovators.

Yes, participants are welcome to stay at the venue for the duration of the event. Think of it like a giant sleepover! Bring your essentials, a sleeping bag, and anything you need to stay comfy for the weekend. We'll provide the space for you!

Teams can have up to four people. Teams will not be assigned, and you can form your team before the event or meet new friends once you arrive. You will have time to find teams at the venue or on the Discord at your own pace!
