00  /  The Event

A student-run hackathon for sharp ideas.

Acuity Hacks is a two-day build sprint organized by CASPA Youth. Built for high schoolers — and open to middle schoolers — participants pick a theme, choose a project format, and go solo or team up to ship a final submission.

A diverse panel of professionals reviews every project, giving feedback and judging across multiple dimensions of the work.
